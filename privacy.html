Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 22, 2026

1. Who We Are

Phoenix Platform ("Phoenix", "we", "us") provides cryptographic infrastructure services including post-quantum digital signatures, MPC wallet technology, and attestation verification. This policy describes how we handle information when you use our website (phoenixrise.io), APIs, and services.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide: When you contact us, request a pilot, or subscribe to updates, we collect your email address, name, and company information. We do not collect payment information until you enter a paid subscription.

Attestation data: When you use our Verify tool or API, attestation data is processed for verification only. We do not store, log, or retain the content of attestations submitted for verification.

API usage: For API customers, we log request metadata (timestamps, endpoints, response codes) for rate limiting and service monitoring. We do not log request payloads or cryptographic material.

Website analytics: We use privacy-respecting analytics that do not use cookies or track individual visitors. We collect aggregate data only: page views, referral sources, and country-level geography.

3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to: respond to your inquiries and pilot requests; provide and improve our services; send product updates you've subscribed to; monitor service health and prevent abuse; and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We do not use your data to train machine learning models.

4. Cryptographic Data

Phoenix's core function involves cryptographic operations. We take the following specific commitments regarding cryptographic data:

Private keys, signing keys, and VaultKey material never leave the hardware security boundary (TEE/Secure Enclave) of the device or infrastructure where they are generated. Phoenix employees cannot access customer private keys.

Attestation verification is stateless. When you submit data to the Verify endpoint, it is processed in memory and discarded immediately after returning the result.

5. Data Retention

Contact information: retained while you are an active customer or subscriber, plus 12 months after last interaction. API metadata logs: retained for 90 days. Attestation data submitted for verification: not retained (processed and discarded immediately).

6. Data Security

We protect information using encryption in transit (TLS 1.3), encryption at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. Our infrastructure runs on AWS with industry-standard security configurations.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting privacy@phoenixrise.io. We will respond within 30 days. If you are in the EU/EEA, you have additional rights under GDPR including data portability and the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority.

8. International Transfers

Phoenix operates globally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via our website and, where applicable, by email to registered users.

10. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at privacy@phoenixrise.io or contact@phoenixrise.io.
